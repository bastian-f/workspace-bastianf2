Instructions

  1. Download the Serial Port Server: Click on the dropdown menu on upper right corner and choose your platform. This will download a small program that establishes a connection to your Senscape Board. Execute it.
  2. Connect your Senscape Board and click on the 'Refresh' icon in the Serial Port Server Widget in the center of this page. Choose the port corresponding to your Senscape Board.
  3. Upload the new version of the firmware by clicking on the 'Reprogram' button.
