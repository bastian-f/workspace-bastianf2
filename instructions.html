Instructions

  1. Download the Serial Port Server: Click on the dropdown menu on upper right corner and choose your platform. This will download a small program that establishes a connection to your Senscape Board. Execute it.
  2. Connect your K-PROX and click Refresh
  3. Your K-PROX should now have appeared in the Serial Port Server Widget below. Select it, please.
  4. Upload the new version of the firmware by clicking on the 'Reprogram' button.
