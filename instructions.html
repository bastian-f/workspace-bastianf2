Instructions

  1. For windows users who have not previously installed the K-PROX drivers: please Download the driver package, unzip it, and execute the exe for you windows version (32 or 64 bits).

  2. All users, please download the Serial Port Server as follows:

    a) Click on the dropdown “XXXX” menu in the upper right corner of the screen and choose your operating system. This will download a small program to connect to your K-PROX.

    b) Execute that program by double clicking on it.

  3. Connect your K-PROX and Refresh

  4. Your K-PROX should now appear in the Serial Port Server Widget below. Please select it.

  5. Click the 'Reprogram' button to upload the latest firmware version for your K-PROX.
